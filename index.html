About the Photographer

Hello, I'm Geethu Nair

I'm a portrait photographer based in Downingtown, Pennsylvania, and I go by @focustoframe on Instagram. Photography, for me, is about more than capturing images — it's about preserving emotions, relationships, and the quiet beauty of ordinary moments.

From golden autumn leaves to the first soft snow of the season, from playful pets to heartfelt portraits — I find magic in every frame. My style is intimate, atmospheric, and always authentic.
