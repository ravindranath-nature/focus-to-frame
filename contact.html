Common Questions

FAQ

How do I book a session? +

Simply fill out the contact form above or send me an email at geethurnair.socsams@gmail.com. Tell me what kind of session you're interested in and we'll find a date that works!

Where are sessions held? +

I'm based in Downingtown, Pennsylvania and shoot primarily outdoors in natural settings. I'm happy to scout locations together or use a spot that's meaningful to you.

How long until I receive my photos? +

Edited images are typically delivered within 1–2 weeks via a private online gallery. You'll receive a link to download all your high-resolution images.

What should I wear? +

Wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable! Solid, earthy tones tend to photograph beautifully. I'll send styling tips after you book.

Do you photograph pets? +

Absolutely — pets are some of my favorite subjects! I have a knack for capturing their personality. You can bring your furry friend along to any session or book a dedicated pet shoot.
